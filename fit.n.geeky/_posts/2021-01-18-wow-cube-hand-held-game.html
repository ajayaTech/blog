The WOWCube System handheld game will immerse you wherever you go. This electronic gaming system comes in the shape of a cube, but it’s much more advanced than the old Rubik’s.

In fact, this handheld game is great for both playing and learning with its 24 high-resolution screens and eight autonomous modules.

What’s more, it has a built-in CPU and multiple magnetic connectors. Made with an open API and SDK, this gadget even lets you develop new games to play. Get new games through the connected app store and enjoy practicing spatial reasoning by playing in 3D.

With eight processors and no buttons, this fully tangible gadget brings joy to the whole family. It’s a stylish, high-tech device that brings mixed-reality gaming to anyone of any age.
