Sony's PS5 release announcement for the Indian market may have come as a long-awaited relief for many gamers in that country.

They will be open on January 12, 2021 on 12:00 IST, and can be made on online platforms such as Flipkart, Amazon.in, Croma and Reliance Digital.

They will also be available through some other retailers such as Vijay Sales and Sony Center.

This long-awaited event will precede the launch of the PlayStation 5 (PS5) in India. This will take place on February 2, 2021.
